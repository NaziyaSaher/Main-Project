Mission & Vision

  • Evidence management is crucial in the field of forensic science. Evidences obtained from a crime scene are important in solving the case and delivering justice to the parties involved.
  • Hence, protecting these evidences from any form of alteration is of utmost important. Chain of Custody is the process which maintains the integrity of evidence.
  • Inability to maintain the chain of custody will make the evidence inadmissible in court, eventually leading to the case dismissal.
  • Digitalization of forensic evidence management system is a need of time as it is an environment friendly model.
  • Blockchains are digitally distributed ledgers of transactions signed cryptographically in chronological order that are sorted into blocks and is completely open to anyone in the blockchain network.
